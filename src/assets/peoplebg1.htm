7 Signs That You're A 'Friends' Addict

Don't worry, "I'll be there for you."
Alyssa Bogus
Alyssa Bogus Mar 21, 2016

1. You hear “How are you doing?” as “How you doin’?”

Joey’s infamous catch phrase to pick up women is “How you doin’?” and every time someone asks how you’re doing, you instantly hear Joey’s voice.

2. You have watched the series multiple times and you still cry at the finale.

SPOILER ALERT: We all knew Ross and Rachel would be together, and that Monica and Chandler would get married and have a family of their own. But I would expect Phoebe and Joey to be together and all of a sudden a new man enters Phoebe’s life and they get married and poor Joey has no one. But somehow they all live happily ever after, and we all still cried because we devoted ourselves to 10 seasons of love, laughs, and friendship.

3. Your heart breaks whenever Ross and Rachel break up.

When they FINALLY get together, they are perfect for each other. But one small mishap and all of a sudden a love filled relationship turns into “We were on a break”. Whatever side of the argument you were on, you still died a little inside when they aren’t together anymore because, face it, they're each other's lobster.

4. Monica and Chandler are everything.

The real life relationship goals here are Monica and Chandler. Friends from the start who eventually fall in love, get married, and even though they can’t have kids of their own, when they find out they adopt not one, but TWO babies; they couldn’t be happier to find a house and have a new life.

5. Phoebe is your spirit animal.

Not caring, singing her heart out, and running like a weirdo through the park; all things we wish we could do but we steer clear of. Phoebe has no cares and does what she wants. She IS an inspiration to all of our inner children. She sings "Friends" addict favorites like “Smelly Cat” and makes us all laugh with her everyday antics, and makes us all wish we could be kids again.

6. “OH. MY. GOD.”

Oh, Janice. Chandler's ex-girlfriend who actually believed he would move to Yemen, gets with Ross, and eventually finds her way back into Chandler’s life just as him and Monica strive to buy a house. The recurring nightmare to Chandler is a fan favorite when she comes back out of the blue with a simple “OH. MY. GOD.” and a horribly annoying laugh.

7. You Wish Central Perk Was Real.

You see the friends meet there in every episode and you always wonder what the coffee is like or how it feels to sit on the infamous couch. You wonder if Central Perk is a real place somewhere and if you got the chance to go there your life would be complete. Well, we can all dream.

11 Reasons You Might As Well Binge-Watch Jersey Shore, If You're Resigned To Watching It At All

You get to see fist pumping and hear "RAHN STAHP."
Kelsey Blaylock
Kelsey Blaylock Jan 24, 2018

In December of 2009, MTV gave us one of the best reality television shows to date, "Jersey Shore." From New Jersey, Miami, to even Italy, we got to witness our favorite (not you Angelina) housemates party, cry, and fist pump. So, if you’re looking for a new Hulu show to watch for hours on hours, "Jersey Shore" is the one for you.

1. Learn how to GTL

Three words. Gym, tan, laundry. The "Jersey Shore" cast lived at the gym, in a tanning bed, and hit up the laundry mat, all in that exact order.

2. Mike, The Ultimate douchebag

You’ve probably never seen a man so obsessed with himself until you meet Mike, “The Situation,” who also causes a lot of situations throughout all the seasons.

3. Watch Sammi “Sweetheart” cry over Ron 72,400 times

A relationship that was set up for failure from the start. You will hear Ronnie call Sammi every name in the book, and then Sammi say, "RON, STAHHHP," to only see her get right back with him. This usually happens once every episode.

4. Snooki’s Hair Bump

With every episode, Snooki’s hair bump rises a little bit. A trend that only she could pull off.

5. The Infamous & Anonymous Letter

JWOWW and Snooki go undercover to let Sammi know about all of Ron’s wrongdoings.

6. Witness Forever Friendships

Pauly D and Vinny have an inseparable bromance, and JWOWW and Snooki are BFFs still to this day.

7. Jersey Shore Slang

DTF: Down to f**k

Smush: sex

Gorilla: A man with large muscles, what all the chicks want on the show.

Guido: A male that is tan, uses a ton of hair gel, and is fit.

Guidette: A female that is tan, gets nails done often, and wears almost no clothing.

Grenade: A term used to describe an ugly female (SO NICE!).

8. Sunday Family Dinner

A day where usually all drama is set aside, and the "Jersey Shore" family bond and eat. It’s mandatory for every cast member to be present.

9. Ron Ron Juice

Invented by the great Ronnie, which he pre-games with just about every time. A nice concoction of vodka, watermelon, and cranberry.

http://vodkaonly.com/how-to-make-ron-ron-juice/

10. Random Fights

Punches are thrown, and hair is pulled by many. What is not to love about that?

11. Night Clubbin’

Where the "Jersey Shore" cast thrives and look forward to all day long.

MTV has recently remade a similar reality television show, "Floribama Shore," but it doesn’t even compare to how amazing "Jersey Shore" was. The "Jersey Shore" cast plans on reuniting in 2018 at some point, which will be the best day ever. Now stop watching “The Office,” for a little bit, turn on season 1 of "Jersey Shore," and take on Seaside Heights, New Jersey with them.
